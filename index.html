Questions, answered plainly

Still unsure where to begin? A short conversation is usually enough to find the right first step.

How much does a website cost?+

Basic is currently 999 PLN and Premium is 1,999 PLN. Optional maintenance is available for 200 PLN per month. We confirm the final scope, timeline and price in writing before work starts.

Can you work with my existing website?+

Yes. We can audit, improve or rebuild your current site depending on what will create the best return.

Do you manage Google Ads after setup?+

Yes. We can provide ongoing management and optimisation, or hand over a clean, documented setup.

Will my tracking be GDPR-conscious?+

We apply data minimisation, consent-aware tagging and secure defaults. For formal legal advice, we recommend a qualified privacy professional.
